About

My name is Abril Betances and I am a graphic design student at Altos de Chavon School of Design. Designing for me becomes more than just fun, it is a challenge that every day makes you a better person in many different aspects. So if you want to know what my creative process is, keep reading...

My Process

The first thing I do when I have a project is to look for information about it, after I have the necessary information I look for the problem and the solution. And that's when it starts to get complicated. The difficult thing is to look for the solution, but everything depends on how you look.

My Approach

I want to have an effective way to find a solution to the problem that is not boring, why should it be boring? If you are already looking to fix a problem, make it fun! life is too short to be boring. We are designers for some specific reason, some of us like the idea of ​​creating something new, others the challenge of finding the solution. All the reasons are valid, but in the end we are designers because it makes us happy what we do.

My Goal

I want to see each project as a new way to have fun and do what I love, sometimes the problem is so big that sometimes we forget why we started.
